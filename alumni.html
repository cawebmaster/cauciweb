Alumni

Since its inception, Chinese Association has been fueled by the passion and dedication of its student leaders. Our board officers have inspired members of the club and the community for over 50 years. It is through their contributions that Chinese Association has grown into one of the largest social cultural organizations at the University of California, Irvine.
