Favorite Memory from Ski Trip
What happens at ski trip stays at ski trip (;
No, I definitely loved just being able to meet so many new faces and getting close to them in that week. I only knew two people when I went and it's honestly just such a great way to meet the people outside of your family. That's where I met Jana!

Favorite Part of Culture Night
The process. I know this sounds cliche, but I really enjoyed being able to see new faces and see everyone be able to let loose and have fun. I was able to relive something that I was passionate about and honestly it's just a really fun experience because of the process and I highly encourage everyone to take part in CN somehow, whether that be through Skit, Dance, or Ninjas!
