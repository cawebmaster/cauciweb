Membership

Chinese Association is always open to new members! Whether you are a college freshman, a recent graduate, or anywhere in between, you are welcome to join us at our next meeting. Find your big, meet your family, and get to know your fellow members and board officers!

Offical membership is only $30 for the entire year and includes:

  • This year's newly designed CAUCI T-shirt
  • Exclusive discounts to select restaurants and buisnesses across Orange and Los Angeles County
  • Discounts to select after-events and social mixers
  • Oppotunities to join our sports, hip hop dance , and Culture Night performance teams
  • Opportunities to join our internship program
  • Opportunities to join our Mentorship program
  • Opportunities to run for a board position for the 2018-2019 school year

While all newcomers are welcome to join our events, only official members have access to the above benefits. Curious? Come out to our next meeting! For questions regarding membership, please email secretary@cauci.com

During the school year, meetings are every Thursday @ 7pm at the Ring Room at the Cross Cultural Center.
